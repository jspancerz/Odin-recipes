Chicken Sandwich

This is a southern favoirte, breaded deep fried chicken sandwich.

The perfect meal on a hot summer day, with a side of cheesey bacon fries!

Ingredients

  • Fresh chicken breast
  • Brioche Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoe
  • Mayo
  • Spicy sandwich pickle (Optional)
    •

    Steps

    1. coat chicken breast in bread crumbs and fry till cooked thoroughly
    2. prepare fresh vegetables, wash and chop as needed.
    3. lightly butter and heat brioche buns
    4. apply chicken to bun with desired toppings
    5. Enjoy!
