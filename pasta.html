Pasta

Perfect dinner for the entire family after a long work week.

Delicous blend of italian flavors with an added kick

Ingredients

  • Fresh angel hair pasta
  • Red sauce of your choosing
  • Fresh Basil
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Meatball
  • Chopped Garlic
    •

    Steps

    1. Boil pot of water and upon reaching boiling point put pasta in water.
    2. Strain pasta and heat red sauce.
    3. Grate cheese and prepare basil by chopping to desired length.
    4. Cook meatballs on frying pan with light drizzle of olive oil.
    5. Add all ingredients to a plate, mix it up.
    6. Enjoy!
