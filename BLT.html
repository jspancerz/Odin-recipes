Deluxe BLT

Whip this delicious meal in a snap

The classic comfort meal, with a spicy twist!

  • Crispy Thick Cut Maple Bacon
  • Lettuce Hearts
  • Tomato Slices
  • Avacado
  • Chipotle Mayo
  • Rustic Italian Bread
    •

    Steps

    1. Fry bacon to desired doneness (best if nice and crispy!)
    2. Wash vegetables and chop tomatoe to desired size
    3. lightly spread butter on bread, lightly pan fry to crisp.
    4. Spread chipotle mayo and assemble with bacon and veggies.
    5. Enjoy!
